Welcome to NOW.TS! This guide will help you get started with collecting and showcasing powerful video and text testimonials.

## Quick Setup

### 1. Create Your Organization

First, create your organization to start collecting testimonials:

1. Sign up for a NOW.TS account
2. Create your first organization
3. Invite team members if needed

### 2. Set Up Your First Collection

Create a testimonial collection to organize your testimonials:

1. Go to your organization dashboard
2. Click "Create Collection"
3. Configure your collection settings
4. Share the collection link with customers

### 3. Collect Testimonials

Start collecting testimonials from your customers:

- **Video testimonials**: Customers record video directly in browser
- **Text testimonials**: Simple form for written feedback
- **Audio testimonials**: Voice recordings for podcasts
